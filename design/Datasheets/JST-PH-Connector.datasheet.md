## Reviewed Summary

- Family / part: JST PH series wire-to-board connectors; the PDF is a family sheet rather than a single exact suffix drawing.
- Geometry: 2.0 mm pitch, top-entry or side-entry options, through-hole and SMT headers, 2-16 positions, with 8 mm top-entry mounting height and 4.5 mm width.
- Electrical: 2 A AC/DC (AWG #24), 100 VAC/DC, -40 to +105 degC, 10 mOhm max initial contact resistance, 1000 Mohm min insulation resistance, and 800 VAC withstand.
- Cable / PCB: AWG #32 to #24, insulation OD 0.5 to 1.5 mm, and applicable PCB thickness 0.8 to 1.6 mm.
- OCR-visible family parts include SPH-004T-P0.5S / SPH-002T-P0.5S / SPH-002T-P0.5L contacts plus SMT headers from 2 to 16 positions.
