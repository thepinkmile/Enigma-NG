## Reviewed summary

### Curated design notes

- PDF review confirmed this is the Wurth Elektronik WE-CMBNC common-mode power line choke, nanocrystalline core, order code 7448031002.
- The schematic on page 1 is a 2-line common-mode choke with one winding between pins 1 and 2 and the second winding between pins 4 and 3. The
  recommended drilled hole pattern is 10.7 mm x 7.5 mm with 1.3 mm holes.
- Core electrical data on page 1: 2 windings, 2 mH inductance at 10 kHz / 0.1 mA, 10 A maximum rated current at 70 deg C, 6.3 mOhm maximum DC
  resistance at 20 deg C, 300 Vac maximum rated voltage, and 2100 Vac insulation test voltage (50 Hz, 5 mA, 2 s).
- General limits called out by the PDF are -55 deg C to +125 deg C operating temperature, MSL 1, AEC-Q200 Grade 1 qualification, climatic category
  55/125/21, and temperature rise below 55 K.
- Mechanical data on page 1 includes a 24.0 mm maximum body length, 17.0 mm maximum body height, 25.0 mm maximum overall length, and the footprint
  dimensions shown above. Page 3 adds the CISPR 17 test setup and the ambient-temperature derating curve.
- Application/reference content is present as attenuation and impedance curves plus the page-5 links to the vendor tutorials "Applications of EMC
  Components" and "Behaviour of Common Mode Chokes." These are useful when using the part as an input EMI choke rather than as a transformer.
- Extraction limit: the wave-soldering profile, attenuation plots, and impedance plots are image-heavy and some rotated labels do not extract cleanly;
  the raw page-by-page extraction is preserved below for local search.
