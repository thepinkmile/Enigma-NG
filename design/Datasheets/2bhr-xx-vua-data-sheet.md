## Reviewed Summary

- Exact family: Adam Tech 2BHR-XX-VUA.
- Product: 2-row box header, vertical PCB mount, 0.100 in / 2.54 mm pitch, gold-flash plated.
  Dual-keyed variant of the BHR-XX-VUA series; polarisation key is present at both ends of the
  shroud to prevent misinsertion from either orientation.

  The 30-pin variant (2×15) is `2BHR-30-VUA`.
- Dimensions / pattern: same formula as BHR series — `A = 2.54 mm × positions / 2 + 7.60 mm`,
  `B = 2.54 mm × positions / 2 + 5.08 mm`, `C = 2.54 mm × number of spaces`.

  5000 Mohm min insulation resistance, 1000 VAC / minute dielectric withstand, -55 to +105 degC.
- Materials: PBT UL94V-0 insulator, brass contacts, gold flash over nickel overall, RoHS compliant.
