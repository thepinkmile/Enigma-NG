# I/O CPLD Mechanical Placement & Assembly

### 1. Chip Placement
*   **Symmetry:** CPLD #1 (Left-side 32 keys) and CPLD #2 (Right-side 32 keys) placed on the rear of the board to allow keys/lamps/sockets on the front.

### 2. JTAG & Data Connectors
*   **Entry:** 10-pin Micro-Fit (From Controller Board).
*   **Exit:** 10-pin Micro-Fit (To Rotor Stator Board).
*   **Manual:** Bilingual ALL-CAPS GERMAN / (Sentence English) labels for JTAG entry/exit.

### 3. Thermal & ESD
*   **ESD:** TPD4E001 arrays near the JTAG and Micro-Fit headers.
*   **Thermal:** Vias under the EPM240 "PowerPad" (if applicable) for heat dissipation.
