JTAG TOPOLOGY (read carefully before flagging anything JTAG-related):
- TCK/TMS/TDI travel via BtB connectors (ERM8/ERF8 Samtec 0.8mm pitch) through the ENTIRE rotor stack — NO ribbon cables carry JTAG within the rotor stack
- JTAG chain terminates at the Reflector; Reflector R1 (22 ohm 0603) is the end-of-chain TDO damping resistor — CORRECT
- Reflector J4 ribbon to Stator J7 carries: pin 15=TTD_RETURN (JTAG), pins 3-14=ENC_IN/ENC_OUT (plugboard config for Stator CPLD — NOT JTAG). The ribbon does NOT extend the JTAG chain.
- Extension U1 buffers TCK/TMS every 5-rotor group because a full rotor stack can be 30 rotors — cumulative BtB capacitive loading requires re-buffering
- Stator R7-R15 (75 ohm) and Encoder R7-R8 (75 ohm) apply to encoder RIBBON CABLE ports (J4/J5/J6) ONLY — they do NOT apply to the BtB rotor stack
- Reflector Design_Spec section 3 JTAG chain END warning blockquote with J4 pin-by-pin breakdown — CORRECT
- JTAG_Integrity.md references to Extension U1, Reflector R1 end-of-chain, BtB vs ribbon topology — ALL CORRECT
